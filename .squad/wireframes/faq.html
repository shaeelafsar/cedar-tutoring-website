What grades do you support?

Cedar supports K-12 students across reading, math, writing, science, homework help, and test prep, with plans tailored to each student's needs.

Do you only help students who are behind?

No. The wireframe assumes FAQs cover catch-up support, confidence building, enrichment, and getting ahead.

How do you decide what my child needs?

Every plan starts with a free assessment and conversation with the family so Cedar can recommend frequency, goals, and program fit.
