# ACT prep that helps students move faster, stay accurate, and feel in control.

Cedar's ACT prep combines content review with pacing strategies so students can handle the exam's speed without losing confidence.

## Ideal For

- Students deciding whether the ACT fits their strengths better than the SAT
- High schoolers who know the material but struggle to finish timed sections
- Students who need a structured plan for English, reading, math, and other tested skills
- Families who want clear guidance instead of piecing prep together on their own

## Focus Areas

- Pacing strategies for moving efficiently without careless mistakes
- Content review for ACT English, reading, and math skills most likely to hold scores back
- Section-by-section coaching so students know when to push, skip, and return
- Practice analysis that turns timing breakdowns into an actionable plan

## Format

- Initial review of student goals, baseline performance, and section-level strengths
- Small-group tutoring that leaves room for questions, correction, and guided repetition
- Timed drills and longer practice blocks that build stamina gradually
- Family updates on priorities, study habits, and next-step recommendations

## Outcomes

- Better pacing across fast-moving ACT sections
- Improved accuracy when students feel pressed for time
- More confidence choosing the right strategies under pressure
- Stronger awareness of which sections need the most practice before test day
- A steadier prep rhythm that reduces last-minute stress
