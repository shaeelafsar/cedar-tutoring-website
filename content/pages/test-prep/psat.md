# PSAT prep that turns an early benchmark into a real advantage.

Cedar helps students use the PSAT as a smart starting point for stronger habits, earlier confidence, and a smoother path toward future SAT goals.

## Ideal For

- Students who want early exposure to high-school test-prep expectations
- Families who see the PSAT as a way to build readiness before the SAT
- Students aiming to strengthen pacing, reading, writing, and math foundations
- High schoolers who want thoughtful coaching before junior-year testing pressure peaks

## Focus Areas

- Foundational reading, writing, and math review tied to PSAT-style questions
- Timed practice that helps students learn the rhythm of standardized testing
- Strategy coaching for staying calm, focused, and organized during the exam
- Mistake analysis that shows where early support can make the biggest difference

## Format

- Assessment-led planning based on current academic strengths and future goals
- Small-group sessions that build comfort with both content and test expectations
- Practice work designed to reinforce habits without overloading younger students
- Guidance for families on how PSAT prep can connect to later SAT planning

## Outcomes

- Greater comfort with standardized testing before higher-stakes exams arrive
- Stronger academic habits in reading, writing, and math
- Better awareness of strengths and growth areas for future prep planning
- More confidence approaching timed work and unfamiliar question formats
- A smoother transition into later SAT preparation when the time comes
